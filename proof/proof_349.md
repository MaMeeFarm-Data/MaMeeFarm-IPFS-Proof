## Description

This proof documents the **early morning temperature and humidity conditions**
at **MaMeeFarm**, recorded on **18 December 2025 at 07:00 AM (GMT+7)**, prior to
the start of daily farm activities.

The image shows a digital temperature and humidity meter mounted against a
brick wall, displaying an ambient temperature of **18.5°C** and relative
humidity of **78%**.

This reading represents baseline environmental conditions before physical
labor begins, providing essential context for interpreting animal behavior,
human work capacity, and subsequent farm activities throughout the day.

The measurement is recorded under natural conditions, without artificial
climate control, reflecting real-world environmental exposure at the farm
site.

This record is captured **as-is**, without adjustment or enhancement, in
accordance with DGCP principles of ground-truth data, temporal accuracy, and
transparency.

The embedded timestamp in the image provides verifiable temporal evidence and
maintains continuity within **MaMeeFarm’s daily proof archive**.

This proof is part of the official **DGCP MaMeeFarm DATA** collection on
OpenSea and is distributed via IPFS (ipfs.io) for immutable reference.
