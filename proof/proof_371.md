# Proof_371 — Daily Inspection of an Empty Duck Shelter
MaMeeFarm | DGCP™

## Description
This proof documents a **daily inspection of an empty duck shelter** at MaMeeFarm on  
**22 December 2025 at 07:21 AM (GMT+7)**.

Although no animals remain, the structure is still visited and observed each day. The absence itself becomes data — evidence of systemic exclusion rather than inactivity.

## Significance
Absence is a form of truth. This proof records persistence despite loss of operational livestock.
