# Proof 389 — Morning Temperature Check

## Context
This proof records the start of daily work at MaMeeFarm™ through environmental measurement.

## Description
At 06:41 on 26 December 2025, ambient temperature and humidity were recorded using a digital thermometer installed on the farm wall.
The measured temperature was 16.8°C, indicating cool morning conditions at the start of the workday.

## Significance
This record serves as a daily environmental baseline for farm activity under the DGCP framework.

## Activity Classification
- Category: Environmental Measurement
- Type: Temperature & Humidity Observation
- Location: MaMeeFarm™, Thailand
- Time: 06:41 (Local)
