# Proof 399 — Environmental Temperature Check Before Work

## Context
This proof documents an environmental temperature and humidity check conducted before starting daily work at MaMeeFarm™.

## Description
On **28 December 2025 at 08:04 (local time)**, a digital thermometer-hygrometer was used to record on-site environmental conditions.  
The display showed:
- **Temperature:** 16.5°C  
- **Humidity:** 74%

The sky was clear with strong sunlight.  
This measurement establishes baseline environmental data prior to any physical labor activities for the day.

## Significance
Environmental measurements provide verifiable context for labor conditions and ground-truth climate data.  
This proof anchors real-world environmental conditions directly from the field without inference or external data sources.

## Activity Classification
- Environmental Observation
- Pre-Work Verification
