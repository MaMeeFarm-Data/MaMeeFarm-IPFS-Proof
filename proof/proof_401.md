## Context
This proof records the dismantling of unused farm structures for basic survival use.

## Description
On **28 December 2025 at 08:11 (local time)**, sections of fencing from the former duck coop were dismantled.  
The reclaimed wood and bamboo materials were repurposed as firewood for cooking.

The action was taken to support daily living needs for both humans and dogs on the farm, following the absence of ducks.

## Significance
This proof documents resource reuse, manual labor, and subsistence decision-making under constrained conditions.  
It reflects adaptation rather than optimization.
