# Proof 403 — Firewood Preparation for Farm Dogs

## Context
This proof documents labor dedicated to animal care through energy preparation.

## Description
On **28 December 2025 at 11:37 (local time)**, bamboo and reclaimed wood were cut and prepared as firewood.  
The firewood was intended for cooking food specifically for the farm dogs at MaMeeFarm™.

No commercial fuel sources or external assistance were involved.

## Significance
This proof anchors unpaid labor, animal welfare support, and biomass energy use within the farm ecosystem.  
It preserves evidence of care-driven labor beyond economic productivity.

## Activity Classification
- Animal Care Support
- Energy Preparation
- Manual Labor
