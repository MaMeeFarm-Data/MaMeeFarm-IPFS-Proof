# Proof 417 — Solar Inverter Power-On

## Context
Restarting household electricity via a solar inverter system after overnight shutdown.

## Description
This proof records the activation of a hybrid solar inverter connected to household power.
The inverter is shut down nightly at 23:00 due to limited battery capacity and restarted in the morning.

## Observed Data
- System: Hybrid solar inverter
- Time: 07:45
- Date: 2025-12-31
- Constraint: Insufficient battery storage

## Significance
Documents real-world energy constraints and infrastructure limitations.
