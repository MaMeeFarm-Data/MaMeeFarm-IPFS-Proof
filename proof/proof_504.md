## DGCP Proof 504

## Context
This proof documents a routine farm maintenance activity conducted at MaMeeFarm™.

and hygiene management.

The activity is classified as routine maintenance and forms part of daily operational
responsibilities required to maintain basic living conditions for animals on the farm.
